Chi Phi

In the Spring of my Sophomore year I joined the Chi Phi Fraternity. A social fraternity on campus that has been at Rutgers since 1864. Here we have worked closely with the University, other organizations, and New Brunswick to give back to the community. Some of the philanthropy work that we do includes working with the Boys and Girls Club of America as well as help the nearby New Brunswick community gardens.

This past summer I created the Chi Phi website as well as a dashboard that allows for the exective board to update information without having knowledge with programming. More information can be found in the project section.
